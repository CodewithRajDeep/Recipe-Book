Banana Pancakes

Fluffy and sweet banana pancakes perfect for a cozy breakfast. Made with ripe bananas and served with maple syrup.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Meal breakfast pancakes Desert

instructions

step 1

Mash ripe bananas in a bowl. Add eggs, milk, and vanilla extract. Mix well.

step 2

Stir in pancake mix until the batter is smooth but slightly lumpy.

step 3

Pour batter onto a heated skillet. Cook until bubbles form, flip, and cook the other side.

ingredients

1 1/2 cups dry pancake mix

2 ripe bananas

1 cup milk

1 tsp vanilla extract

tools

Mixing Bowl

Whisk

Non-stick Pan

Measuring Cups
